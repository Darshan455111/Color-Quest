How to Play Color Quest

1

Setup: 24 pawns of 6 hidden colors (4 of each) are shuffled and placed randomly in holes around the circular board. Nobody knows their arrangement!

2

Step 1: On your turn, click the Roll Die button in the center. The die will land on one of the 6 colors.

3

Step 2: Select any occupied hole on the board to lift its pawn and reveal the hidden color on its bottom face.

4

Match: If the pawn color matches the die color, you collect the pawn, gain 1 point, and get an extra turn immediately!

5

Mismatch: If they don't match, the pawn stays revealed for 2 seconds (remember its location!), returns to the board, and the turn passes to the next player clockwise.

6

Victory: When all 24 pawns are collected, the game ends. The player with the highest score wins! Ties are possible.
